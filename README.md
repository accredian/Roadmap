## 🎯 Why These Roadmaps?

Generic roadmaps tell you what tools to learn. **Accredian Roadmaps** tell you how to build a **career**. We focus on:

  * **The Transition Matrix:** Moving from "Doing" (Coding/Modeling) to "Leading" (Strategy/Architecture).
  * **Skill Sequencing:** What to learn first to maximize your market value.
  * **Leadership Milestones:** Identifying the non-technical skills (Stakeholder Management, ROI Analysis) needed at every level.
